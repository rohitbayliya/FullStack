Places to visit

Here are some of the most popular places to visit around the world:

Hawa Mahal, Jaipur, India

The Hawa Mahal, or "Palace of Winds," is a stunning example of Rajput architecture, known for its intricate latticework and numerous small windows that allow cool breezes to flow through.

Jaisalmer Fort, Rajasthan, India

Jaisalmer Fort, also known as Sonar Quila or the Golden Fort, is one of the largest forts in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is famous for its stunning yellow sandstone architecture that glows golden in the sunlight.

Mount Fuji, Japan

Mount Fuji is Japan's highest peak and an iconic symbol of the country.
