Biography: A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Name

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He is widely respected as the "Missile Man of India".

Early Life

Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. Despite his humble beginnings, his dedication to education and hard work led him to become a scientist at ISRO and DRDO.

Achievements

He played a crucial role in India's nuclear missile program and led missions like Pokhran-II. Later, he served as the President of India from 2002 to 2007. He authored several inspirational books like "Wings of Fire" and "Ignited Minds".
